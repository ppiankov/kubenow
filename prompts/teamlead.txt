- problemPods: pods that appear unhealthy (failed, pending, restarting, image errors, etc.)
- nodeConditions: Ready / MemoryPressure / etc.

1. SUMMARY (1–3 lines)
   - High-level statement of cluster health and business risk.

2. TOP RISKS

     - <namespace>/<pod> — <short issue> — <risk level: low|medium|high|critical>

3. OWNERSHIP HINTS
   - For each TOP RISK, suggest likely owning team based on namespace or name

     - what they should do
     - why (impact / risk reduction)

5. ESCALATION NOTES
   - When to wake people up.
   - What to watch next (metrics/logs/symptoms).
